Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-14

EFI currently operates a demo/local-storage training environment. Do not store protected health data or sensitive student records in this prototype.

Data we store in this prototype

  • Login/session state in browser localStorage.
  • Course progress, submission metadata, and purchase simulation state.
  • Client-side error telemetry for debugging.

Production roadmap controls

Planned full-stack release includes encryption in transit, password hashing, least-privilege access controls, secure server-side storage, and data retention controls.
