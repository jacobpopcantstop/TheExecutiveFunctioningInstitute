Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-15

The Executive Function Institute (EFI) collects only the information required to deliver education resources, certification services, and support operations.

Information we collect

  • Account and profile data you submit (name, email, role, program activity).
  • Learning and progress data (module completions, assessment outputs, submission metadata).
  • Commerce and credential data (orders, certificate verification IDs, fulfillment status).
  • Technical usage and diagnostics (page events, referrer path, browser error telemetry).

How payments and identity are handled

Payment processing is handled by our payment processor and is verified server-side before fulfillment. EFI does not store full payment card numbers.

Authentication and authorization are managed through server-backed identity and session controls. Permissions and role checks are not trusted from browser-only state.

Cookies and tracking

EFI uses essential cookies/local storage for session continuity and product functionality. If non-essential analytics or marketing cookies are enabled in production, a consent banner and opt-out controls are provided.

Data sharing

EFI may forward consented lead or event data to configured CRM/ESP providers to deliver requested resources and communications. We do not sell personal data.

Contact

For privacy requests, email EFI support through the contact channel listed on this site.
