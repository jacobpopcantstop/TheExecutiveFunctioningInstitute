How It Works

From Enrollment to Practice

1

Enroll & Get Immediate Access

Upon enrollment, you get immediate access to all 6 modules, your complete Starter Kit, the interactive ESQ-R assessment, and all downloadable resources. No waiting, no drip content.

2

Study the Modules & Use the Tools

Work through the 6 modules at your own pace, using the Starter Kit templates in practice sessions. Each module builds on the last — from neuroscience foundations through hands-on coaching methodology.

3

Complete the Capstone & Get Certified

Submit your three-part capstone practicum: an intake simulation packet, a case study intervention plan, and an original coaching resource. Pass rubric review, earn your CEFC designation, and launch your practice.
