Program Details

Program Overview

A flexible, rigorous path to professional certification designed for working adults and career changers.

The CEFC program is structured to accommodate diverse schedules while maintaining the depth and rigor necessary for competent practice. The self-paced format allows you to move through material at your own speed while preserving clear accountability through assignments, unit tests, and rubric feedback.

  • Format: Fully asynchronous and self-paced
  • Duration: Approximately 6 months
  • Certification: Certified Executive Function Coach (CEFC)
  • Renewal: 2-year validity with CEU requirements
  • Philosophy: Open-source, science-based

Who This Is For

Educators, mental health professionals, parents and caregivers, and career changers who want to build a practice grounded in the neuroscience of self-regulation — not generic life coaching platitudes.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the CEFC certification program.

No specific degree is required to enroll in the CEFC program. The curriculum is designed to be accessible to motivated learners from a variety of professional backgrounds. That said, backgrounds in education, psychology, counseling, social work, or related fields are beneficial and may allow you to move through certain material more quickly. What matters most is a genuine commitment to understanding the neuroscience of executive function and applying it ethically in practice.

Yes. The program is delivered entirely online in a fully asynchronous format. Modules, readings, assignments, unit tests, and capstone submissions can all be completed on your own schedule without required live sessions.

The Certified Executive Function Coach (CEFC) credential is valid for 2 years from the date of issue. Renewal requires the completion of continuing education units (CEUs) to ensure that certified coaches stay current with evolving research and best practices in executive function coaching. Renewal requirements and approved CEU sources are provided upon certification.

The Professional Launch Kit is a comprehensive suite of assets designed to help you start your coaching practice immediately upon certification. It includes assessment tools (including the ESQ-R), session agenda templates, intervention planning worksheets, client agreement templates based on ICF standards, a business plan framework, a service menu template, and marketing resources. Think of it as the "toolbox" you carry into your first professional engagement.

The CEFC program aligns with the professional and ethical standards established by the International Coaching Federation (ICF) and the National Board for Executive Function Coaching (NBEFC). Our curriculum is designed to meet or exceed the competency frameworks outlined by these organizations. While accreditation processes vary by jurisdiction, our commitment to open-source, evidence-based education ensures that graduates are trained to the highest standards of the field.
